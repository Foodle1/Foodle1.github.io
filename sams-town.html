Project Overview

This project was made as a team of 4 as a serious game for Awesome Women in Construction. The purpose of this was to inspire more women to join the construction industry. We did this by showcasing women in all parts of the construction industry through mini-games.

This project was made in Unity and programmed in C#. Creating multiple mini-games allowed me to work on many different things within the same project. It allowed me to further my knowledge of object-oriented programming within games by creating bases for systems that would be inherited in different ways in each mini-game.

  • During this project, I undertook the following roles/tasks:
  • Lead Programmer
  • Playtesting
